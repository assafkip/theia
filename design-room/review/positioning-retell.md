# Floor — positioning-retell (FORK — founder owns positioning)

The page's spine is the one-sentence retell a visitor repeats. Candidates (fork):

- **A — "It drops what it can't prove."** Spine = honesty/subtraction. The dropped-
  count is the hero. Ownable, contrarian, matches the founder's moral spine.
- **B — "Every fact carries the sentence that proves it."** Spine = provenance/receipts.
  The source-span is the hero. Positive framing of the same mechanic.
- **C — "Threat PDF in, structured intel out — grounded."** Spine = the transform.
  Clearest for a first-time visitor, weakest at differentiation.

fork: the founder picks the spine. It decides the hero copy and the subject. NOT a
lens decision. (Recommendation is NOT the tool's to make — surfaced as fork B vs A
leading, C as the safe/legible floor.)
